We are A.C.G.

The Aggie Consulting Group is a non-profit, student-run organization that strives to provide undergraduate students the opportunity to solve real-world business problems for clients. We strive to provide our clients with high-quality deliverables, new perspective, and strategic recommendations while granting our consultants the valuable leadership and professional development experiences that accompany professional-level interactions with companies.
